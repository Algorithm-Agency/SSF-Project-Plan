Section 01 · Your Project

What we are building for you

A fully custom 6-page WordPress website for Sta-Sof-Fro — no premium base theme, built from your approved Figma designs. This project covers the website build only. A monthly development retainer is available afterwards as an optional add-on, but is not part of this project or its pricing.

Why a custom build

No premium base theme — the theme is built from scratch to match your brand identity and approved Figma designs precisely. That means no bloated code, no conflicting plugin dependencies, and a site that's fast and easy to maintain going forward.

What this project delivers

Full UX/UI design across all 6 pages, a custom WordPress theme, all pages built in Elementor, three CF7 forms with validation and routing, and full QA before go-live. A monthly retainer is available afterwards, entirely optional.

Payment & go-live policy

The mock-up stage is free and comes before any invoice — you approve the direction before committing to anything. From Phase 2 onward, each of the 3 paid phases is invoiced upfront. We do not start work on a phase until that phase's invoice is paid in full, and invoices are never raised retroactively for work already done. This applies to go-live too — the completed site stays on staging until Invoice 3 clears, so there's no scenario where the site goes live before payment is received.

Section 04 · Revision & Change Management

Managing scope and changes

1 round of revisions is included per design phase. Everything beyond that is a change request that must be scoped and approved in writing before any work begins.

Included as a revision

  • Layout and spacing adjustments
  • Colour and font changes
  • Copy edits and image swaps
  • CTA placement or wording
  • Minor reordering of existing sections

1 round included. One consolidated feedback list — no drip-feeding.

Treated as new scope

  • Change in direction after a round is approved
  • New pages or sections not in the original scope
  • A fundamentally different approach to a page
  • Additional revision rounds beyond the one included

Must be scoped, priced (R750/hr), and approved in writing before any work begins.

Change request process

Client raises a request → Ashlin assesses hours and cost → Written approval from client before any work starts → Invoice raised on completion.

Communication protocol

Single point of contact: All client communication goes through Ashlin. Luke does not communicate with the client directly under any circumstances.

Response SLA: 24 business hours for all queries.

Scope changes in writing: No additional work is actioned until the client has confirmed in writing.

Section 07 · Working With Us

How to work with us

A few things that make a big difference to how smoothly the project runs. None of this is complicated — it is just how we work best together.

What we need from you to get started

  • Signed quotation and 40% deposit (R37,000)
  • Figma designs confirmed as final
  • Final written copy for all 6 pages
  • Product and lifestyle photography
  • Brand guidelines and logo files
  • Kick-off meeting confirmed

How to give feedback

  • We share designs or builds for your review at the end of each phase
  • Send feedback as one consolidated list — not separate emails over several days
  • Be specific: "change the button colour to navy" not "something feels off"
  • 1 round of revisions is included per design phase
  • Any new requirements beyond the agreed scope are scoped and quoted separately before we action them

How we communicate

  • All communication goes through Ashlin — one contact, always
  • We respond within 24 business hours to all queries
  • We do not action scope changes without written confirmation from you
  • We flag anything that may affect the timeline before it affects the timeline

After launch

  • Your site is fully signed over to you on receipt of final payment
  • A monthly retainer is available afterwards — entirely optional
  • We'll check in post-launch to see if ongoing support makes sense for you
  • No pressure either way

What a successful project looks like from our side

A site that reflects your brand precisely, a client who knows exactly what is happening at every stage, and the option — never the obligation — of ongoing support once you're live. That is what we are building toward.
